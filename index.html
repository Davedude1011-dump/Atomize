Welcome to Atomize, a science game designed and produced by Thomas Smallwood
This is a Hydrogen Atom, its the first you have collected
dew to it being the first on the Periodic Table. The aim
of the game is to get to the Final Atom Ununoctium,
this is how to play.

At the top you will see a progress bar, if you click the
atom it will contribute towords it. (stop trying to click
it now your in the tutorial! :I)

The amount of electrons an atom has will determin how many
points it will give per click, Hydrogen = 1, Helium = 2,
Ununoctium = 118.

But if it was just that it would be boring so after obtaining
Lithium (3), a WormHole will apear at your screen, you can
spend the ThermoNuclear Energy that you gain by tapping atoms
all day to get cool buffs and rewards :). Goodluck and GoodDay.
